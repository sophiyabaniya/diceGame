Game of DICE Rules

About the game:

The rules are......

There are two players:(Player-1 and Player-2).

The players can roll the dice turn by turn. Whoever rolls a 'one' or 'six' in the dice, gets 1 point added to his/her scoreboard. The total score is 5. Hence, the player who achieves a score of 5 first, wins!
